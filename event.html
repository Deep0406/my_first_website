Major Events & Festivals in India

Experience the vibrant cultural and religious events celebrated across India.

Lohri

Punjab’s harvest festival celebrated with bonfires and folk songs.

Date: January 13, 2025 | Evening

Kumbh Mela

World’s largest religious gathering, held every 12 years.

Date: January 14 - March 10, 2025 | Time: All day

Pongal

Tamil Nadu’s major harvest festival.

Date: January 14-17, 2025 | Time: All day

Goa Carnival

A grand celebration with parades, music, and dance.

Date: February 22-25, 2025 | Time: Afternoon & Evening

Holi - Festival of Colors

Holi is a vibrant festival known for colors, music, and joyous celebrations.

Date: March 25, 2025 | Time: Morning to afternoon

Eid-ul-Fitr

Muslims celebrate Eid with prayers and feasts after Ramadan.

Date: April 1, 2025 | Time: Morning prayers

Bihu

Assam’s New Year and harvest festival.

Date: April 14-16, 2025 | Time: Day & Evening

Rath Yatra

Date: July 4, 2025 | Morning

Jagannath temple festival in Odisha featuring grand chariot processions.

Raksha Bandhan

A festival celebrating the bond between brothers and sisters.

Date: August 10, 2025 | Time: Morning & Afternoon

Onam

Kerala’s harvest festival, known for boat races and feasts.

Date: September 7-17, 2025 | Time: All day

Durga Puja

West Bengal’s grand festival honoring Goddess Durga.

Date: October 1-5, 2025 | Time: Morning & Evening Aartis

Navratri

Nine nights of worship and Garba dance in Gujarat.

Date: October 3-12, 2025 | Time: Night (Garba Dance)

Diwali - Festival of Lights

Celebrated across India, Diwali marks the victory of light over darkness.

Date: November 12, 2025 | Time: Evening till late night

Guru Nanak Jayanti

Date: November 15, 2025 | All Day

Sikh festival celebrating Guru Nanak’s birth with prayers and processions.

Pushkar Camel Fair

Date: November 15-23, 2025 | All Day

A traditional fair in Rajasthan featuring camel races and cultural performances.

Christmas

Celebration of Jesus Christ’s birth across India.

Date: December 25, 2025 | Time: Midnight & Morning Prayers
